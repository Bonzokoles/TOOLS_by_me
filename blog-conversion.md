## Email Sequence

### Welcome Series (5 emails)
```
Email 1: Dostarcz lead magnet + intro
Email 2: Case study / success story
Email 3: Value tip + soft pitch
Email 4: Objection handling
Email 5: Hard pitch + scarcity
```
